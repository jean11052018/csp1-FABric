About Us

Mission

Our mission is to spread our love of fabrics to everyone that's interested. Sharing information about where fabric all begun and where it's heading. A place where everyone can share their experienced and at the same time help everyone where to start and where to get affordable materials.

Vision

We want to be the leading platform that caters to forums about fabric and a place where everyone finds an affordable but quality fabric for their projects. We will also encourage everyone to showcase their works to inspire people.
